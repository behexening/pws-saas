About akFISHinfo.

Real-time commercial salmon opening alerts for Prince William Sound.

What it is

ADF&G opening announcements are PDFs buried in an email list. By the time word gets around, the window's already closing. akFISHinfo parses the announcement the moment it hits, updates the map, and texts you.

Who it's for

Commercial salmon fishermen in Prince William Sound. Permit holders, tender operators, crew — anyone who needs to know what's open and where the line is, before everyone else finds out.

How it works

1
ADF&G issues an announcement
Alaska Department of Fish & Game emails a PDF opening announcement to a monitored inbox.
2
PDF is parsed automatically
The attachment is processed by an AI parser that extracts district names, gear types, open/close windows, and any special constraints.
3
Map updates instantly
The interactive district map reflects the current opening status for all PWS districts in real time.
4
SMS alert goes out
Pro subscribers receive a text message immediately — no refreshing, no checking, no delays.

The team
