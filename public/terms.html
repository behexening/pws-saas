2 — Description of Service

akFISHinfo monitors a designated email inbox for opening announcements issued by the Alaska Department of Fish & Game ("ADF&G") for Prince William Sound commercial salmon fisheries. When an announcement is received, the Service uses automated AI-assisted parsing to extract district information, gear types, and open/close windows, and delivers SMS alerts to active subscribers.

The Service is an information delivery tool, not an authoritative source of fishing regulations. All alert content is derived from official ADF&G communications. akFISHinfo does not generate, author, or modify regulatory information.

The Service is intended to provide timely access to information that ADF&G has already made public. It is not a substitute for direct monitoring of official ADF&G channels.
